About Agra

Agra, the city of romance, is home to the magnificent Taj Mahal — an eternal symbol of love. Situated on the banks of the Yamuna River, Agra enchants visitors with its blend of history, architecture, and culture. From grand Mughal forts to peaceful gardens, it offers travelers a glimpse into India’s royal heritage.

Top Attractions



Taj Mahal

The marble masterpiece built by Emperor Shah Jahan for Mumtaz Mahal, symbolizing eternal love.

Agra Fort

A UNESCO World Heritage site showcasing Mughal strength and beauty with red sandstone grandeur.

Fatehpur Sikri

The ghost capital of the Mughal Empire, filled with stunning palaces and ancient charm.

Mehtab Bagh

A tranquil riverside garden offering picture-perfect sunset views of the Taj Mahal.
