About Mathura & Vrindavan

Mathura and Vrindavan, located in Uttar Pradesh, are sacred twin cities deeply associated with the life and stories of Lord Krishna. Mathura, his birthplace, resonates with spiritual energy, while Vrindavan is where he spent his childhood. The cities are adorned with temples, ghats, and vibrant festivals like Holi and Janmashtami, attracting pilgrims and tourists from across the world.

Top Attractions to Visit

Shri Krishna Janmabhoomi

The birthplace of Lord Krishna, this sacred temple complex is the spiritual heart of Mathura.

Banke Bihari Temple

One of Vrindavan’s most famous temples dedicated to Lord Krishna, filled with divine devotion.

Prem Mandir

A stunning marble temple showcasing beautiful carvings and illuminated night views.

Yamuna Ghat

Enjoy the tranquil evening aarti on the banks of the sacred Yamuna River, a divine experience.
