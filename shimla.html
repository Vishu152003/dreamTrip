About Shimla & Manali

Shimla, the capital of Himachal Pradesh, is known as the ‘Queen of Hills’ with colonial charm, mall roads, and scenic mountain views. Manali, nestled in the Beas River Valley, is a paradise for adventure lovers offering snow-capped peaks, temples, and river rafting. Together, they form one of India’s most loved hill station circuits, offering beauty, peace, and thrill in equal measure.

Top Attractions to Visit

The Ridge, Shimla

Heart of Shimla city, offering panoramic mountain views and colonial architecture.

Mall Road

Famous for shopping, cafés, and heritage walks along the picturesque hills.

Rohtang Pass

A must-visit snow-covered gateway connecting Manali to the Lahaul–Spiti valleys.

Hadimba Temple

An ancient wooden temple surrounded by cedar forests dedicated to Goddess Hadimba Devi.
