About Rishikesh

Rishikesh, located in Uttarakhand, is a serene town nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas. Known as the ‘Gateway to the Garhwal Himalayas,’ it is a spiritual hub famous for yoga, meditation, and adventure sports. Whether you seek inner peace through the Ganga Aarti or thrill from river rafting, Rishikesh offers a perfect blend of calm and excitement.

Top Attractions to Visit

Laxman Jhula

A legendary suspension bridge offering stunning views of the Ganges and surrounding hills.

Triveni Ghat

Experience the divine evening Ganga Aarti, a mesmerizing blend of lights, chants, and devotion.

River Rafting

Feel the adrenaline rush as you conquer the rapids of the sacred Ganges River.

Beatles Ashram

A tranquil ashram once visited by The Beatles, now a colorful graffiti-filled spiritual retreat.
