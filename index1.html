Frequently Asked Questions

Your travel queries, answered with love ✈️🌍

How does DreamTrip generate travel plans?
DreamTrip uses smart filters & travel preferences such as budget, duration, and interests to suggest personalized destinations & activities.
Do I need to create an account to save my trip?
No. DreamTrip uses local storage so you can save, view & delete travel plans without logging in.
Can I customize my trip after generating it?
Yes! You can edit preferences anytime and generate a fresh plan instantly.
Is DreamTrip free to use?
Yes — DreamTrip is completely free and always will be.
Can I plan trips outside India?
For now, DreamTrip focuses on popular destinations across India. International trip planning is coming soon.
