About Ayodhya

Ayodhya, one of the seven sacred cities of India, is located on the banks of the Sarayu River in Uttar Pradesh. It is renowned as the birthplace of Lord Rama and holds deep religious and historical significance. With the grand Ram Mandir standing tall, Ayodhya welcomes millions of devotees and tourists each year to experience its peace, devotion, and culture.

Top Attractions to Visit

Ram Mandir

The newly constructed grand temple dedicated to Lord Rama — a symbol of devotion and Indian heritage.

Hanuman Garhi

A 10th-century temple devoted to Lord Hanuman, offering breathtaking views of Ayodhya city.

Sarayu River Ghats

Peaceful riverbanks perfect for spiritual reflection and evening Aarti ceremonies.

Kanak Bhavan

A beautiful temple dedicated to Lord Rama and Sita, adorned with gold and intricate carvings.
