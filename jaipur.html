About Jaipur

Known as the Pink City, Jaipur is the capital of Rajasthan and a vibrant fusion of royal heritage, culture, and architecture. Founded by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II in 1727, Jaipur is home to magnificent palaces, bustling bazaars, and breathtaking forts. From the intricate Hawa Mahal to the majestic Amer Fort, every corner of the city tells a story of regal beauty and tradition.

Top Attractions to Visit

Hawa Mahal

The iconic ‘Palace of Winds’ built in 1799, designed for royal women to view the city without being seen.

Amber Fort

A majestic hilltop fort with stunning courtyards, mirror work, and panoramic views of Jaipur.

City Palace

A blend of Mughal and Rajasthani architecture, housing museums and royal collections.

Jantar Mantar

An astronomical observatory built in the 18th century, now a UNESCO World Heritage site.
